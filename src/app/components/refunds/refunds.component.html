Refund & Cancellation Policy

We encourage all users to thoroughly explore the course details, curriculum, and instructor information before making a purchase. It is essential to ensure that the course aligns with your learning goals and expectations.

5. Changes to Course Content:

6. Exceptions:
As mentioned earlier, the only exceptions for a refund would be those required by law. In such cases, we will abide by the relevant regulations and process the refund accordingly.
