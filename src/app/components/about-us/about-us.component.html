Art and Psychology for Healing and Creativity

Mona Joy: Psychologist & Art Therapist

Meet Mona Joy, a talented and versatile professional with a unique blend of expertise in psychology and the arts. With a Bachelor of Fine Arts (BFA) from the esteemed Government College of Art in Chandigarh and a Master's in Clinical Psychology (MA) from IGNOU, Mona's educational journey laid the foundation for her multidimensional career.



As an Art Therapist and Practitioner certified by both the American Art Therapy Association (AATA) and the Art Therapy Certification Board (ATCB), Mona has mastered the art of using creativity to heal and empower individuals. Her specialized training in Autism assessment and treatment further enhances her abilities to work with diverse populations.
